Featured Cocktails

Beach Blossom

A tropical delight, Beach Blossom combines premium rum with exotic fruits and floral notes, capturing the essence of a beachside paradise in every sip.

Fiery Maiden

Is a bold and adventurous cocktail featuring a blend of spicy tequila, tangy lime juice, and a hint of fiery jalapeño syrup. This vibrant drink promises to ignite your senses with each sip, embodying the spirit of a daring and passionate maiden.

Grilled Peach & Rosemary Prosecco

A sophisticated blend, Grilled Peach and Rosemary Prosecco features the smoky sweetness of grilled peaches and the aromatic touch of rosemary, all perfectly balanced with sparkling prosecco.

Green Envy

Green Envy is a tantalizing cocktail that combines crisp gin with refreshing cucumber, mint, and a hint of lime. This vibrant green drink offers a cool, invigorating taste that's perfect for any occasion.

Blackberry Mojito

A refreshing twist on a classic, the Blackberry Mojito combines fresh blackberries, mint, and lime with premium rum for a vibrant, fruity cocktail bursting with flavor.

Drunk Patriot

Inspired by the colors of the Ghana flag, the Drunk Patriot is a visually striking cocktail featuring red grenadine, yellow pineapple juice, and green Midori. This vibrant and layered drink celebrates bold flavors and pays homage to Ghana with every sip.
